# Privacy Policy for Suraksha

**Effective Date: April 22, 2026**

Suraksha ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information We Collect
Suraksha is a personal safety tool that requires access to specific sensitive data to function during an emergency.

### A. Real-Time Location Data
- **What we collect**: Precise geographical location (GPS and network-based).
- **Purpose**: To provide your real-time coordinates to your designated guardians when an SOS trigger is activated.
- **Background Access**: The App requires "Allow all the time" location access to ensure that emergency tracking works even if the App is closed or the screen is locked.

### B. Contacts
- **What we collect**: Phone numbers and names of the contacts you manually select as "Guardians."
- **Purpose**: To identify the recipients of emergency SMS alerts and automated calls. We do not upload your entire contact list to any server; data is stored locally on your device.

### C. SMS and Phone Services
- **What we use**: The App uses your device's SMS and Calling capabilities.
- **Purpose**: To send automated text alerts with your location and initiate emergency calls to your guardians when an SOS is triggered.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used **strictly for emergency response services**. We do not:
- Use your location for advertising or marketing.
- Sell or lease your personal data to third parties.
- Track your location unless a safety protocol (SOS, Journey, or Geofence) is actively enabled by you.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- **Local Processing**: All sensitive information, including your guardian list and emergency logs, is stored locally on your mobile device.
- **No Cloud Storage**: Suraksha does not maintain a central server for user data. Your location is shared directly from your device to your guardians via SMS.

## 4. Permissions Disclosure
To provide sub-second emergency response, the App requests the following:
- **Location**: To find you during an emergency.
- **SMS/Call**: To alert your guardians.
- **Post Notifications**: To keep the safety shield active in the background.
- **Battery Optimization**: To prevent the OS from disabling the safety service during idle periods.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
